About Me

FAQ

How much do you charge?

If you are interested in working with me, definitely send me an email and we can talk through everything! For reference, here is a general range:

  • Concerts: $150-$300
  • Athletics: $200-$500
  • Client Work: $250-$600

These prices are heavily determined by the work necessary for the proposed shoot. For example, a full-day outdoor Track Meet would cost more than a single indoor Basketball Game.

Where did you go to school?

I went to Highschool at CBHS (Class of 2019), then went to Rhodes College (2024) and double majored in Computer Science and Media Production. I also took a Gap Year during the height of Covid, where I took online classes from MTSU in Video Editing, Photography, and other similar courses. I also have a certificate in Content Production from Rhodes.

How many pictures do you provide?

This depends on the event, but I always share at least 30, typically more.

Who have you worked with?

I've worked with a number of Memphis partners such as Unapologetic, the Overton Park Shell, the Memphis Grizzlies, Memphis River Parks, and Grind City Meet! I've also worked with individual clients like Royal Studios, Mak Ro, and Dirty Streets.

Can you do Video?

Yes! I have experience editing content, directing music videos, and filming entire concerts too! At this time I am not willing to commit to this work alone due to a lack of equipment, but I am more than willing to do video as part of a team.

Favorite thing to shoot?

I've always been a music fan, so concerts are my go-to. I think it's what I'm the best at, and I'm always the most proud of my work following concerts. I also love shooting inside recording studios when I get the opportunity.
